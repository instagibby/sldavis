About Me

Hello I am Spencer Davis, 24, and from Iowa. I have many experiences with computers that all started with gaming when I was young. That love for games and the internet flourished into a desire to make my own sites and games. I got on the internet and learned a bit of Javascript and Python to then start making a game through the Unity Engine using C#. After that eye opening experience and the satisfaction it brought me I decided to change course from my Associates of Arts through DMACC to get myself a Web Developement Diploma (finishing Spring 2018) and to pursue a career with UI/UX and making video games in mind.

I'm 24 years old and learning how to build my own websites and program my own games. Currently I know how to use HTML, CSS, C#, Python and JavaScript. Why I do what I do

I've grown up playing lots of video games in my free time. I regularly used the internet my entire childhood and because of the amount of time I have spent around computers and in general just using them it gave me the drive and desire to make my own. Often I will use a website for a product or restaurant to try and navigate through their information but I will be turned away by a slow moving, lackluster site. This is something I want to be able to help create so that not only will it function, but function well and effectively. That is my goal when I make sites.
